Stagewise regression is a form of monotone lasso regression, in that it doesn't allow a sudden change in curve direction. A beta value can only be increased monotonically. The magnitues of beta increase with each iteration, or remain stagnant. Stagewise regression doesn't penalize beta values. The stagewise regression therefore tends to take fewer iterations than the lasso regression and shows a lower error.
